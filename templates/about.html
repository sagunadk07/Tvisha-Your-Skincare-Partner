About Us

Your Skincare Partner, Powered by AI

TVISHA was built to make skincare simple — no more guessing which products actually work for your skin.

What We Do

TVISHA uses an EfficientNet-B0 deep learning model to analyze photos of your skin across 8 different conditions — from acne and redness to pigmentation and pores. In seconds, you get a clear picture of your skin's current state.

Personalized Recommendations

Once we know what your skin needs, we map those results to specific ingredients proven to help — like Salicylic Acid for acne or Niacinamide for oil control — along with real product suggestions to try.

Why We Built This

Most people don't have access to a dermatologist for everyday skincare questions. TVISHA bridges that gap with accessible, AI-powered analysis — so everyone can make informed choices about their skin.
